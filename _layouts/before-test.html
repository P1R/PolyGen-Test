How To Book Your Test

Simply go to book online and select the test you require then follow the steps. At any booking screen (with the exception of PCR Test Only and site tests) you will have to enter basic details before you can select your test type.

PCR International Arrivals Day2 Day8 (Amber Countries) (Home Test)

You will need to complete your remaining details and pay. You will be sent a confirmation email and receipt. Please check your Junk box for the confirmation email.

Before Your Test

You should await your selected delivery day. All orders for Day2 and Day 2 & 8 are dispatched by 24 hour courier to you on your Day 0.

You should self-swab using the instructions provided

You should then log in to your online dashboard if you have received log in details and then activate your kit or follow the instructions in your Kit Activation email if you booked a site test or a PCR Test Only.

You should then return your test to our lab using the return packaging and 24 Hour Tracked label provided post into a Royal Mail Priority Box. You can find your nearest box here and check collection times: https://www.royalmail.com/services-near-you#/

Please place in a box with a collection time scheduled just after you post.
You will receive your results within 24 Hrs of receipt at lab by email. We now offer site testing too.
